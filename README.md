# AlertsApp – Rocket & Missile Alert Statistics

[English Version](#english-version) | [גרסת עברית](#hebrew-version-גרסת-עברית)



## Purpose

This app was created to provide Israeli citizens with statistical insights into rocket and missile alerts, showing which hours are most dangerous and when alerts are most likely. It is **not** a live warning system, but a tool to analyse trends and patterns from past alerts.

The app calculates per-hour statistics, highlights the peak danger hours, and shows summaries and visual charts. It helps citizens understand the likelihood of alerts during specific times, based on historical data.



## Features

* Display alerts by city and category
* Highlight dangerous hours (most alerts) in red
* Highlight safe hours (least alerts) in green
* Provide per-hour statistics with percentages
* Visualise alerts in bar charts
* Filter alerts by date range
